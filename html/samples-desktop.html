Your Custom Home Page

Casting directors and industry professionals will first encounter your home page - a central hub with navigation to your "About" page, work credits, demo reel, and photo gallery. Front and center, you'll find your Headshots capturing attention and giving casting directors instant access to your look and range. Click "Contact" and your Contact Card appears instantly, while clicking your Resume button immediately downloads your resume—both without leaving this page. At the top and bottom of the page, you'll find Dynamic Photo Galleries that slowly scroll through your images and stills from your projects. Industry professionals can click the Social Media Bubbles for direct access to your Instagram, IMDb, and other profiles.
