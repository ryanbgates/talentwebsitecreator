FAQ

Who can join the Referral Program?

Only clients who have purchased a website are eligible. Once your site is created, you'll automatically receive your referral code.

How do I get paid?

You earn $50 for each referral once they complete their final payment and receive their fully completed website. Referral earnings are paid out through PayPal twice per month on the 1st and 15th.

Can multiple discount codes be used?

No — only one referral code can be applied per purchase. The code must be entered when making the initial deposit. The $100 discount applies to their final payment, and you earn your $50 once they complete that final payment.

When will I get my code?

Your referral code becomes available immediately after your website purchase. This Referral Program page will transform into your "My Referrals" page, where you can view and share your unique code.
